Analysis of the Count

In the end this count did not play out as projected by the calculator. First, instead of Legalise Cannabis being ahead of the Greens by around 3,000 late in the count, the impact of below the line preferences meant the Greens were ahead by around 1,500. Secondly, the Liberal Party had not elected its second MLC by this point, so the distribution of Legalise Cannabis preferences left both the Greens and Liberals short of a quota. The exclusion of 8,472 first preferences for Stuart Grimley of Derryn Hinch's Justice Party were required to put Mansfield over quota, electing her to the fourth vacancy, while Liberal Joe McCraken was elected to the fifth and final vacancy.
