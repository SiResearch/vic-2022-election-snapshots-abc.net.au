Analysis of Count

At the very end of the preference distribution, Liberal Trung Luu defeated DLP candidate (and ex-Liberal) Bernie Finn by just 210 votes. It was a close race determined in the end by the composition of the surplus votes for the successful Legalise Cannabis candidate David Ettershank. How close the race was also came down to the fluke of Green preferences on their own being enough to elect Ettershank. That left another 5,356 votes received by the Greens as preferences to be distributed before Ettershank's surplus was dealt with. These votes resulted in Luu having a further net gain of 170 votes over Finn. It was a very close finish.
